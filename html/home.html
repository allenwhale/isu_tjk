Introduction

2015 Taiwan/Korea/Japan (TKJ) Joint Meeting on Chemical Engineering will be held from November 5-7, 2015 in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. This conference started in 1994 and held biennially on a rotating basis among Taiwan, Korea and Japan. The 2015 TKJ Joint Meeting will be organized by the Department of Chemical Engineers at I-Shou University in Taiwan. This conference provides opportunities for the delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences, to provide a platform for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals to deliver the latest innovative research results, to share the most recent development and trends in chemical engineering, and to find global partners for future collaboration. Authors from the world are welcome to gather at this international academic event.

Topics

The conference topics should be related but not limited to:

A. Biochemical and Biomedical Engineering
B. Transport Phenomena and Separation Technology
C. Thermodynamics and Interface Technology
D. CO2 Capture and Utilization
E. Material Science
F. Electrochemical Technology
G. Green Chemical Technology and System Engineering
H. Catalysis and Reaction Engineering
TJK1. Energy and Environmental Engineering
TJK2. Bio/Nano Materials

The topics mentioned above are all welcome to share their common interests and passion about chemical engineering.
